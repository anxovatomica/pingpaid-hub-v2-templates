📊 Annual Review Template

Reflect on your year · Plan next year · Updated June 2026

Most freelancers never review their year. They just keep working. This template helps you reflect, learn, and plan.

💰 Financial Review

Income Analysis

Total Revenue:
$_______ (vs. $_______ last year = _______% change)
Profit (Revenue - Expenses):
$_______ (_______% margin)
Average Project Value:
$_______ (_______ projects total)
Hourly Rate (if applicable):
$_______/hour (_______ hours billed)
Largest Client (% of revenue):
_______% (Danger: over 40% is risky)

👥 Client Review

Client Analysis

New clients:
_______ (Sources: _______)
Repeat clients:
_______ (_______% retention rate)
Lost clients:
_______ (Why? _______)
Problem clients fired:
_______ (Good. Fire more if needed.)
Referrals received:
_______ (Goal for next year: _______)

🎯 Skills & Growth

Professional Development

New skills learned:
_______
Courses completed:
_______ ($_______ invested)
Certifications earned:
_______
Tools mastered:
_______

🚀 Goals for Next Year

Set 3-5 Specific Goals

Income goal:
$_______ (_______% increase)
Rate increase:
_______% (New rate: $_______)
New clients:
_______ (_______% from referrals)
Skills to learn:
_______
Work-life balance:
_______ hours/week max, _______ weeks vacation
💡 Pro tip: Do this review every December. Share it with an accountability partner. Review progress quarterly. What gets measured gets improved.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to handle late payments?

The best approach is automated invoice collection with clear payment terms, gentle reminders, and professional follow-up sequences. Tools like PingPaid can automate this entire process for you.

How do I calculate late fees on invoices?

Late fees are typically calculated as a percentage of the overdue amount (usually 1-2% per month). You can use our free late fee calculator or let PingPaid handle calculations automatically based on your configured terms.

What should I include in a freelance contract?

A solid freelance contract should include: payment terms, late fee clauses, scope of work, revision limits, kill fees, and intellectual property rights. PingPaid offers free contract templates in our template library.
