🌍 Geographic Adjustments

Clients in these regions typically pay these premiums (relative to global averages):

  • US (San Francisco, NYC): +40-80% above global average
  • US (Other major cities): +20-40%
  • UK (London): +20-30%
  • Western Europe: +10-20%
  • Australia: +15-25%
  • Eastern Europe: -20-40% (but quality is equal)
  • Asia (India, Philippines): -40-60%
  • Latin America: -30-50%
💡 Pro tip: Charge what US clients pay regardless of where you live. Remote work means location shouldn't matter. I charge the same rate to San Francisco clients as I do to Barcelona clients.

📈 Rate Increase Strategy

  • New clients: Charge your new rate immediately
  • Existing clients: Give 60-90 days notice. Most won't blink.
  • Retainer clients: Increase 10-15% annually (standard)
  • Project clients: New projects = new rates
  • Good clients who push back: Offer 6 months at old rate, then new rate
  • Bad clients who push back: Let them go. You'll replace them at your new rate
